Additional Project

Motor Lift Bracket

Developed bracket concepts for safely lifting and handling propulsion hardware while balancing stiffness, packaging, and ease of use on the shop floor.

Overview

This was a focused hardware problem around fixture-like functionality: support the real handling loads, simplify use during assembly or service, and keep the design practical to fabricate.

What I Built

  • Lift-bracket concepts sized around realistic handling loads
  • Packaging and interface studies for the surrounding hardware
  • Iterative design refinements for fabrication and useability
