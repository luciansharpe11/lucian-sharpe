Additional Project

Smart Pillbox Product Development

Designed and prototyped a smart pillbox combining enclosure design, PCB electronics, and embedded control into a manufacturable proof-of-concept system.

Overview

This project focused on integrating mechanical packaging, embedded electronics, and user-facing hardware into a compact prototype. The work emphasized practical design iteration, feature reduction, and simplifying the path to manufacture.

What I Built

  • Enclosure and mechanism concepts for a connected pillbox device
  • PCB and embedded control integration around an ATmega-based system
  • Prototype iterations that reduced manufacturing complexity
