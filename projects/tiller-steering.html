Tiller Steering Prototype Development

Flux Marine intern project focused on developing a tiller steer MVP, combining mechanical design, electrical integration, and software coordination into a functional prototype.

Overview

This Flux Marine intern project centered on rapid design-build-test cycles to develop a tiller steer MVP and prove out steering functionality, package constraints, and subsystem integration. It also produced drawings and documentation that supported continued development.

What I Built

  • Prototype tiller-steer hardware and MVP integration concepts
  • Mechanical-electrical-software coordination for functional testing
  • GD&T drawings and development documentation
